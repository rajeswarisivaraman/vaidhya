📱 Install on Android (Chrome)

1
Open index.html in Chrome on your Android phone (you can copy it to your phone and open in Chrome, or host locally)
2
Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right of Chrome
3
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4
Tap "Add" when prompted. The app icon will appear on your home screen.
5
Open from home screen — it now runs fullscreen like a native app, no browser bar visible.

💻 Simplest local hosting option

1
If you have Python installed: open terminal in the app folder and run python3 -m http.server 8080
2
On your phone (same WiFi), open Chrome and go to http://[your-computer-ip]:8080
3
Install from Chrome as above. The app caches itself for fully offline use after first load.
